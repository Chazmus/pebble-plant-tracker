🌿 **Pebble Plant Tracker** is a premium, offline-first companion application designed to manage your indoor garden directly from your wrist. Running on the modern Alloy (JavaScript) engine, it displays your plants in a sleek, high-contrast dark theme showing the relative time since their last care event (e.g. `W: 3d ago | F: 12h ago`). You can instantly log eight maintenance actions (Watering, Fertilising, Repotting, Pruning, Rotation, Cleaning, Treating, and Relocating) and review up to 20 historical logs in a scrollable timeline on the watch.

Configure your plant list, planting dates, and view a complete history timeline through the phone's Pebble settings dashboard. Thanks to watch-side persistent storage and robust AppMessage serialization, your data remains secure and synced even when offline. Compatible with Pebble Time 2 (`emery`) and Pebble 2 / Core (`gabbro` / `diorite`).
